About Tikitakes

Founded in 2020, Tikitakes began as a small family business with a simple mission: to create the most refreshing and delicious lemonades using only the finest, locally-sourced ingredients.

Every glass is hand-squeezed to order, ensuring maximum freshness and flavor. We believe that great lemonade should be simple, natural, and made with love.
